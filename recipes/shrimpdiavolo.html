Shrimp Fra Diavolo

Shrimp Fra Diavolo: Juicy shrimp simmered in a hearty, spicy tomato sauce with a perfect kick!

Ingredients:

  • Large Shrimp
  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Chopped Yellow Onion
  • Minced Garlic
  • Dry White Wine
  • A Can of fire-roasted tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Dried Oregano
  • Chopped Fresh Parsley

Steps:

  1. Heat 2 tbsp of olive oil in a skillet, add shrimp to skillet and season with a pinch of salt. Partially cook for one minute and then remove shrimp from skillet and set aside.
  2. Add 3 tbsp of olive oil to a skillet on medium heat and add chopped yellow onion. Cook for a few minutes and then add minced garlic. Cook for a few more minutes.
  3. Once onions are slightly translucent, add 1 cup of dry white wine and simmer on medium heat until it is reduced to half.
  4. Add the remaining ingredients: fire-roasted tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano and parsley (to taste).
  5. Let sauce simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. Add shrimp to skillet and cook for a couple of more minutes or until shrimp is pink.
  7. Ready to serve with your choice of pasta! (angel hair, or linguine recommended)
  8. Enjoy!
