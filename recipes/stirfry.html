Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry

Chicken and Vegetable Stir Fry: The perfect simple, healthy, yet mouth-watering recipe!

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Fresh Grated Ginger
  • Chopped Yellow Onion
  • Minced Garlic
  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • Coconut Aminos or Soy Sauce
  • Broccoli
  • Sliced Red and Yellow Bell Pepper
  • Sliced Carrots
  • Raw Honey

Steps:

  1. Add 3 tbsp of olive oil to a skillet on medium heat and add chopped yellow onion. Cook for about 5 minutes until onions are slightly translucent and then add minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook for a few more minutes.
  2. Cut chicken breast into cubes and add to skillet with coconut aminos or soy sauce until almost fully cooked.
  3. Remove just the chicken and add vegetables and coconut aminos or soy sauce to skillet until almost fully cooked.
  4. Add chicken breast back into skillet with vegetables to finish cooking.
  5. Add more coconut aminos or soy sauce, red pepper flakes, and 1 tsp of raw honey to taste.
  6. Ready to serve over a bed of rice!
  7. Enjoy!
