Black Bean Tacos

Vegetarian Black Bean Tacos: These delicious tacos are packed with the perfect balance of flavorful black beans, pico de gallo, avocado, and fresh cilantro!

Ingredients:

  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Can of Black Beans
  • Chopped Yellow Onion
  • Minced Garlic
  • Ground Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Avocado
  • Diced Red Onion
  • Diced Tomato
  • Fresh Diced Jalapeno
  • Freshly Squeezed Lime Juice
  • Chopped Cilantro
  • Sliced Avocado
  • Sour Cream (optional)

Steps:

  1. Add 3 tbsp of olive oil to a skillet on medium heat and add chopped yellow onion. Cook for about 5 minutes until onions are slightly translucent and then add minced garlic.
  2. Add rinsed can of black beans to skillet along with 2 tsp of cumin, 1 tsp of paprika, and salt to taste.
  3. Add 1/2 cup of water to skillet and let beans simmer for 10 minutes (or longer if you prefer the beans to have a more mushy consistency.)
  4. Take skillet off heat when beans are at desired consistency.
  5. Prepare pico de gallo by combining red onion, tomatoes, jalapeno, cilantro, lime juice, and salt & pepper to taste. Set aside.
  6. Build your tacos using tortillas of choice, cooked black beans, pico de gallo, and slided avocado.
  7. Optional: top with more cilantro, red onion, lime juice, and sour cream.
  8. Ready to enjoy!
